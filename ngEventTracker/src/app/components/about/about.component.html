What is this thing for?


I am impatient when it comes to food prep, and had fallen into a habit of eating fast food nearly everyday. This is not only incredibly unhealthy, but also expensive. To help get away form this habit I got ahold of an instant pot and began to make large meals that I could simply reheat throughout the week, in particular soups.

In further effort to cut costs and save time I began to experiment with using frozen vegetables and other minor variations to instant pot recipes I found online. Unsurprisingly, these sometimes worked out well, othertimes nearly resulted in an entire pot thrown out.

This program is meant to record the changes made to recipes found online to help remember which things work, and to display the costs as motivation to continue saving money.
